iceberg-dark - view on GitHub

Theme for various programs, designed with love for iceberg.vim theme. Meant to bring iceberg.vim to other environments and to replace it in places, where original theme is hard to read.

Available for lightline.vim, vim-airline, tmux and bumblebee-status.
