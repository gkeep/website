Translations of various tweaks and programs to Russian.

Current list of translations consists of:

  • Starship - The minimal, blazing-fast, and infinitely customizable prompt for any shell!
  • Zebra - Package Manager for jailbroken iOS devices. It is built to support iOS 9 and up for iPhones and iPads alike.
  • Sileo - A modern package manager for iOS 11 and higher.
  • Nougat - Experience Android right on your iOS device! Nougat brings the Notification Panel from Android Nougat and Oreo.
  • Snowboard - Snowboard is a lightweight spiritual successor to the legendary Winterboard theming engine.
  • Dawn - Customize dark and light mode. Dark elements in light mode and vice versa.

If you want me to translate a tweak or an app to Russian, contact me via my email.

iceberg-dark - view on GitHub

Theme for various programs, designed with love for iceberg.vim theme. Meant to bring iceberg.vim to other environments and to replace it in places, where original theme is hard to read.

Available for lightline.vim, vim-airline, tmux and bumblebee-status.
